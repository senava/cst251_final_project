Racing Game Genre

Racing video games are a video game genre in which the player participates in a racing competition. They may be based on anything from real-world racing leagues to fantastical settings. They are distributed along a spectrum between simulations and simplified arcade-style racing games. Point is, you drive something and hope that you can beat your friends, right? Of course it is! Racing games have been around since video games were first made, obviously they look way better now, but thats not to say they havent evolved. Racing games take many form, from racing with cars, to boats, off-road vehicles, and even space ships in some games!

Stuff you might find interesting:

Racing games are probaly the easiest games to pick up when playing with big groups of people, perfect for those that dont play video games, and even kids! There are some notable games such as Forza Horizon 4 which focus on (let's pretend that their Lego expansion doesn't exist for the sake of this argument) the realistic aspect of racing and have a ton of fine tuning features for your cars. Or games like Mario Kart which is based in a universe of fictional characters who use powerups to stump their opponents while racing. These games, just like another, require some basic understanding of the games physics and movement to get good at it.
